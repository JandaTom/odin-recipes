Mayonnaise

This is recipe for a mayo, that can be enjoyed with wide variety of other things.

Ingredients

  • Egss
  • Dijon mustard
  • Fresh lemon juice
  • Garlic clove
  • Salt
  • Vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients except the oil in a container.
  2. Pour in the oil down the side of a container, so it creates a layer above created mixture.
  3. Start blending from bottom up to create a thick and homogenous mixture.
  4. Store your mayo in air-tight container for up to 2 weeks.
  5. You made it! You have made a homemade mayo to enjoy with vegetables, meats or anything else your heart and stomach desire!
